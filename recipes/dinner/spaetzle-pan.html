Spätzle Pan

INGRIDIENTS

  • 500g eggspätzle
  • 400g chicken breast
  • 250ml light cooking cream
  • 1 onion
  • 20g More Protein Cream
  • 30g pizza cheese light
  • 1 tbsp garlic
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 1 tbsp pepper

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Cut chicken and onion and give it to the pan.

  2. Pour the cream and water into the pan. Spice into the pan.

  3. Add protein and Spätzle to the pan. Done.
