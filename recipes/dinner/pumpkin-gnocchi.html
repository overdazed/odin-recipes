Vegan High-Protein Pumpkin Gnocchi

INGRIDIENTS

  • 250g pumpkin
  • 50ml soy milk
  • 150g soy flour
  • 150g wheat flour
  • 25g nutritional yeast
  • Â½ tsp nutmeg
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 1 tbsp pepper

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Puree the pumpkin with the soy milk and place in a bowl.

  2. Add the remaining ingredients and knead into a smooth dough.

  3. Cut the dough into boiling water; when the gnocchi float to the surface, they are done.

  4. Now fry in a little oil and stir in cheese, cream cheese, and a bit of soy milk. Done.
