Chicken Rice Pan

INGRIDIENTS

  • 250g long grain-lace rice
  • 400g chicken breast
  • 1 red pepper
  • 1 onion
  • 1 vegetables bouillon cube
  • 500ml water
  • 20g More Protein Cream
  • 1 tbsp garlic
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 1 tbsp pepper

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Cut chicken, pepper and onion and give it to the pan.

  2. Add vegetable bouillon cube, water and rice in the pan.

  3. When the rice is done, add protein and spices to the pan. Done.
