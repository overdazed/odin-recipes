Apple Pancakes

  • INGRIDIENTS

    • 1 apple
    • 2 eggs
    • 60g spelt flour
    • 30g More Protein Cream
    • 200ml almond milk
    • 7g backing powder
    • 3g Chunky Flavour Vanilla Perfection

    INSTRUCTIONS

    1. Cut the apple into slices.
    2. Stir eggs, flour, protein, backing powder, chunky flavour and almond milk in a bowl.
    3. Preheat the pan and dunk the apple slices in the mixture and fry them in a pan.
